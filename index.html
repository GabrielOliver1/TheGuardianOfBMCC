A MMP-100 Fall Semester Student Presents:

The Guardian of BMCC

    By Gabriel Oliver

A young student of BMCC named Nada Smartee is devastated by a dream that foretells him of a small meteor that is headed straight for his college campus. No one else knows of this information but luckily no one else knows of the powers that dwell in him either. He knows what he must do. Will Nada be able to save his beloved campus or will his degree end up in flames? Find out in the “GUARDIAN OF BMCC”.
